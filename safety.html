Check Weather Conditions

Always verify mountain forecasts before departure. Weather changes rapidly in Karakoram and Himalayan ranges.

Acclimatize Properly

Always rope up on glaciers and steep terrain. Regular equipment checks prevent accidents on technical sections.

Emergency Response Procedures

For altitude sickness: Immediately descend 500-1000 meters. Administer oxygen if available. For fractures: Immobilize the injury using splints. For hypothermia: Remove wet clothing, provide warm drinks, and use body heat.

Helicopter rescue signaling: Use colored clothing or signal mirrors to create contrast. Make triangular patterns on snow. Emergency contacts should be readily accessible.
